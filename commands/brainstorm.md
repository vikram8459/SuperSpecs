**Preflight:** Confirm the `openspec/` workspace root exists; brainstorming will produce `openspec/changes/<change-id>/design-notes.md`.

Use the `spx:brainstorming` skill to refine the user's idea into a design through Socratic questioning, then hand off to `spx:openspec-propose` once the design is approved.
